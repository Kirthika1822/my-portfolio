LoRa-Based Fisherman Border Alert System

IoT · Embedded

PROBLEM

Fishermen frequently drift across maritime borders without warning, risking safety and legal consequences, with no low-cost, long-range way to track boats at sea.

APPROACH & STACK

Built a real-time GPS tracking and border-detection system using an ESP32 microcontroller, GPS module, and LoRa for long-range, low-power communication between boats and shore stations.
